# Field Crosswalk — Control Testing Workpaper

Field-level mapping from Archer to Optro for the standard control testing workpaper variant. This is the granular complement to `workpaper-types.md` — that catalog says *which* variants exist; this crosswalk says *what fields* exist inside this specific variant and what's happening to each.

**Scope of this crosswalk:** Standard control testing workpapers — regular controls, not analytics-based testing and not workpapers using a defined standard audit procedure. Those variants get their own crosswalks.

**Status:** Draft — to be populated from the existing crosswalk document. Validation activities (see end) must be completed before treating as final.

---

## Disposition definitions

| Disposition | Meaning | Implications |
|---|---|---|
| **Keep** | Field maps to an equivalent field in Optro with no material change to meaning or use | Configuration mapping; no methodology or training change needed |
| **Transform** | Field maps to Optro but with a structural change (combined fields, split fields, different format, different picklist values) | Training implication; possible methodology language update |
| **Remove** | Field exists in Archer but is not being carried forward to Optro | Reporting team must confirm no reports rely on it; methodology must confirm the field isn't required by procedure |
| **New** | Optro field with no Archer predecessor | Methodology likely needs language addition; training needed |
| **Deferred** | Decision pending — field is present in both systems but disposition not yet decided | Logged as open item until resolved |

## Cross-references generated by this crosswalk

When the crosswalk is populated, the dispositions generate entries in other artifacts:

- **Remove rows** → `handoffs-reporting-needs.md` "Existing reports to preserve" table for any field that's referenced by current reports
- **Remove rows** → `methodology-impacts.md` for any field referenced in procedures
- **Transform rows** → `handoffs-training-implications.md`
- **New rows** → `methodology-impacts.md` (likely needs language addition)
- **Deferred rows** → `decisions-log.md`

This crosswalk is therefore a *generator* of other artifact entries — populating it creates work elsewhere, and that's the value. A field-level crosswalk is the most concrete place to spot cross-team implications.

## Validation activities

These must be complete before this crosswalk is treated as a deliverable.

- [ ] **Confirm coverage** — every Archer field used by control testing workpapers in current practice is represented (validate against 3–5 recent control test workpapers, not just the field list)
- [ ] **Confirm Optro mappings** — every Keep and Transform row points to a real, configured Optro field (not an assumption)
- [ ] **Reporting team review** — Reporting team confirms no reports rely on Remove rows, or flags ones they need preserved
- [ ] **Methodology team review** — Methodology team confirms Remove and New rows are consistent with what procedures require
- [ ] **Practitioner review** — at least one Senior Auditor and one Team Leader review the crosswalk to confirm no field they actually use is missing

Once all five are complete, capture sign-off in `sign-off-log.md` per the standard defensibility criteria.
